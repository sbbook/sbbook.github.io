We are not affiliated with Tencell or any other Slap Battles staff member or developers. This wiki is also unofficial and is fan-made.

Slap Battles is a game on Roblox created by Tencelll, it is a game about slapping using a different variety of gloves you can use to slap other players. The more slaps you have, the more gloves you can use. There is also badges in the game where you can obtain, in order to get some gloves! Good luck, and have fun browsing here too.
